The Pervasive Edge Intelligence Laboratory (PEILab), directed by Prof. Song Guo, is developing novel and efficient deep learning architectures, algorithms, and systems over ubiquitous mobile, IoT, and wearable devices.

We focus on the following research areas

  • Federated Learning & On-Device Learning
  • Cloud-Edge Hybrid Cyberinfrastructure for Foundation Models
  • Trustworthy AI Governance
